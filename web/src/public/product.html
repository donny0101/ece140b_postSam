theAxe
Who's it for:

Guitarists, Musicians, and Engineers looking to for a cheaper alternative to expensive pedals and software.

What It Does:

An effects pedal with everything you love about digital software. With theAxe webapp, you can design preset effects racks to get right into play and save them to the preset foot switch. There's also a looper to create jams live to play against, in addition to a free foot switch to program with effects as you see fit for use with the angular sensitive

How It Works:

As easy as plug in and play. You can even line out to a digital interface for easy recording and further engineering!

Features:

- 3 programmable foot switches programmable for complete user control - 1 angular sensity foot modulation pedal - 1 line in , 1 line out - Cloud support so presets and pedal presets can be modified and are always ready to go - Fluid digital to analog control to enhance live play or studio sessions through effects modulation
