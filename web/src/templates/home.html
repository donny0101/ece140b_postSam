Laws of UI Design

To satify Hick's law, we have kept the number of buttons and information on the app limited to keep customers from becoming distracted with complex options. To satify the law of Proximity, we have the basic carProtect information displayed, with the additional features on the bottom left side of the screen with the emergency buttons on the bottom right side. We have kept similar features grouped together. In combination with the law of Proximity, to satify Miller's law we have kept the amount of information on both the webapp and the eventual android app to have as little information as needed on screen while still providing all the functionality we want. We can do this by placing larger operations or features as buttons and grouping them as we did.
